[ CONTEXT: 100 years in the future… ]

[ The boy is entering the room.. ]

[ The advanced research lab deep inside the hacker group’s headquarters was quiet, filled with people focused entirely on their computers. Amar, a senior security researcher for the last 2 years, was working deep within the system architecture, trying to engineer a new type of virus with AI capabilities that could bypass modern firewalls. Months ago, Amar had specifically requested this cadet to work under him. For his own unspoken reasons, the strict researcher had built a strong, watchful bond with him. To the boy, Amar was a demanding but trusted mentor. ]

Amar: UGH… WHAT is it that you want right now? Always creating troubles, and NOW WHAT?!

Boy: OHHH God… One of my friends got CAPTURED!! I need your help to save him back!!

Boy: UGH… It’s.. ITSSS…

Boy: It’s near the margin of the GRAVEYARD!

Amar: WHAAAAT?! WHAT did you just say?! Near the “GRAVEYARD”???

Boy: YES! Near it, but not that deep into it!

Amar: UGH! Now you make me MORE angry! NEAR the Graveyard?! OR INTO the Graveyard?!

Boy: [ AVOIDING EYE CONTACT, GRIPPING THE EDGE OF THE DESK TIGHTLY TO HIDE HIS SHAKING HANDS ] Its in the margin of it, i don’t know the perfect longitude and latitude…

Amar: Do you understand WHAT kind of serious situation are you in? That place is graveyard because it is one. It is strictly prohibited for citizens under our protection!

Boy: I know!

Amar: And you said your friend is captured, and you knew it… then you were there too?

Boy: Yes, that is how i knew he captured.

Amar: OHHH man… Its out of our league, those who went into it, never came back.

Boy: UGH… We didn’t go deep! We just wanted to research the geometry of the margin… to find the truth about that strange place! But then that truck came out of nowhere… Please help mee!

Amar: WHAAAAT?! You went there to play researcher? There is no way anyone should go into it. And you and your pathetic friend got caught on your own. Now WHAT do you expect from me?

Boy: I don’t know, we gotta bring him back. He is my friend and I owe him so much!!

Amar: [ THINKING… TAKING THE MIKE NEAR TO HIS COMPUTER SCREEN ]

––––––– AMAR: HELLO EVERYONE!! –––––––

––––––– THE KID HERE LOSES HIS CIVILIAN FRIEND IN THE GRAVEYARD!!! –––––––

––––––– GROUPS: WHAAAAT?! GRAVEYARD?? HOW? WHY SOMEONE LIKE A CITIZEN WENT THERE? HOW COME? –––––––

––––––– AMAR: LISTEN PEOPLE, IF YOU CANNOT HELP, YOU CAN DO YOUR JOB.. –––––––

––––––– GROUPS: FINE!! I’LL DO MY JOB! –––––––

––––––– AMAR: [ SMIRKING QUIETLY, EXPECTING THIS RESPONSE ] WHAT??? –––––––

––––––– GROUPS: WE DON’T WANNA TAKE A RISK. BOSS WOULD BE FURIOUS IF WE TOUCH “GRAVEYARD”! –––––––

––––––– AMAR: I KNOW, I KNOW, I KNOW. BUT THE CAPTURED PERSON IS THIS KID’S FRIEND! –––––––

––––––– GROUPS: OHHHHH… POOR KID. –––––––

––––––– AMAR: UGH… THIS WON’T WORK. –––––––

********** MIC MIC CHECK!! THIS IS KERNEL DIVISION 8 SPEAKING. **********
[ The announcement mic is suddenly ON and a Kernel is on the line ]

KERNEL: Hello people, we as a group never ever back off from any challenges, wheather it is a survival or helping. Amar, as per your request, we understood the problem and planning to solve this in smooth way. Please bring the person in.

Boy: WHAAAAT?! AMAR!! WHAT did you do?

Amar: I knew they wouldn’t help. I was just playing along. So, as a last resort, I pushed a private comms request exactly where it had to go.

Boy: AHHH! That’s awesome Amar. Let’s go, I WILL BRING MY FRIEND BACK!

Amar: Hold your horses!! First we have to face the consequence of your actions.

Boy: Agreed!!

[ BOTH OF THEM WENT IN. ONLY AMAR KNOWS WHAT IS HAPPENING. EVERYONE IN THE GROUPS WERE SILENT AND AHH. ]

––––––– GROUPS: How come Amar bring this news in? He must have some bond with KERNEL. He is the best here anyway… –––––––

[ Both of them went in, Amar knew exactly WHAT he needs to do. ]

Amar: Wait here!! I’ll have a word with KERNEL.

Boy: Yes, okay. Please hurry up!

Amar: All right kid, I said, WAIT!

[ Amar turned around and went to speak with KERNEL before he can get to the kid ]

KERNEL: AHHH, Amar!! It’s been a long time. How are you?

Amar: I’m fine. I need to meet the BOSS.

KERNEL: WHAAAAT?! That urgent, hah!! UNDERSTOOD!

[ KERNEL MAKES A CALL AND IS WAITING FOR THE HIGHERUPS TO PICKUP AND GET THE CONFIRMATION TO ALLOW ]

KERNEL: You can now enter Amar! Bring him in. Boss is waiting for you.

Amar: OHHH! Thanks KERNEL! I never thought process will be this quicker in recent times.

KERNEL: All thanks to BOSS and you, Amar.

Amar: I am just doing my job, you know!!

[ SPEAKING OF WHICH, AMAR TURNED AROUND AND CALL THE KID AND START WALKING TOGETHER INSIDE ]

Amar: Hey kid, come on!! Let me show you someone you dreamed of!!

Boy: Coming!! Is everything okay? When is the rescue team dispatching?

Amar: UGH… You still have a long way to go, kid. We have to meet the BOSS to discuss further.

Boy: [ SHOCKED ] WHAAAAT?! BOSS??? No way, Amar. You have influence with BOSS?

Amar: Well!! You’ll see.

Boy: But I cannot meet the BOSS right now, I am not gonna meet him with the failure in my hand!

Amar: Only a failure will bring success, maybe this failure can change!

Boy: But..

Amar: No buts.. if you wanna save your guy, this is the only way.

Boy: Then, I will do it, NO MATTER WHAT!

[ Both of them approach the massive steel door to the Root. A quiet voice scanner activates. Amar says his specific access code. The door glows green. “Proxy clearance validated. Welcome to the ROOT,” the voice says. The heavy doors slide open smoothly, and both of them enter into the BOSS ROOM ]

Amar: May I Come In, BOSS?

Vali: Yes come in!!

Amar: Hey Vali, how you been?

Vali: AHHH! Long time, No see man!!

Boy: [ THINKING ] WHAAAAT… why everyone is speaking to Amar like he is their friend? am i missing something???

Vali: OHHH! this is the kid you spoken off.

Boy: [ SHOWING PROPER RESPECT ] Hello sir, I am Rudra. Nice to meet you.

Vali: Hi Rudra, welcome to BOSS’s place.

Amar: Where is BOSS?

Vali: Out in the balcony!! As he always.

Amar: Yeah! Right.

Boy: [ THINKING ] OHHH… So Amar do have deep connection here that makes him realize the BOSS usually in the balcony, hah!!

Vali: Both of you wait here, i will call on BOSS.

Amar: UNDERSTOOD!

[ Both of them sat in the near big sofa. Vali walks toward the balcony but hesitates for a second. Through the glass, the BOSS is standing silently, staring deep into a massive, ancient, and majestic painting covering the entire balcony wall. It is a painting of the cosmic dance of destruction—the fierce, wild dance of the ancient god Shiva. Even 100 years into the future, surrounded by cold technology, the Boss draws his deep thoughts from this powerful, cultural energy. Vali watches him for a moment with quiet respect. ]

[ AFTER A FEW MINUTES, BOSS ENTER INTO THE PLACE. ]

[ THE HALL WITH 4 PEOPLE: RUDRA, AMAR, VALI, BOSS ]

Amar: [ STANDING UP ] Hello boss!!!

BOSS: AHHH, Hey, Amar! Its been a long time, isn’t it?

Amar: Yes sir!!

BOSS: Hah!! where this formality comes from? hah.

Amar: BOSS, this is RUDRA.

[ BOSS AND RUDRA MEETS ]

RUDRA: Hello BOSS, I am RUDRA, Terminal Cadet.

BOSS: [ SILENT ] [ BOSS’S EYES LOCKED ONTO RUDRA. IN THAT SINGLE, QUIET SECOND, EVERYONE IN THE ROOM COULD FEEL THE HEAVY WEIGHT OF A MILLION CALCULATIONS RUNNING THROUGH HIS MIND. HE WAS A THINKER INSIDE A THINKER. YET, HIS FACE REMAINED COMPLETELY CALM AND KIND. ]

BOSS: Hi RUDRA, nice meeting you. Hope your work and wealth is going well!!

RUDRA: Everything is good, BOSS. Thanks for the concern. I have a problem and I need your help.

Amar: Calm down, RUDRA!! We are here to think of it.

BOSS: Its okay amar, let him talk.

[ BOSS WALK ON AND SIT ON HIS OWN CHAIR - THE BLACK PANTHER, VALI SAT ON HIS OWN, AND AMAR AND RUDRA SAT ON THE SAME SOFA ]

RUDRA: [ BEFORE EVERYONE SITS ] OHHH God… One of my friend got caught, BOSS!! PLEASE SAVE HIM BACK!

Amar: RUDRA…

RUDRA: My friend got caught in graveyard, i need your help BOSS. He is my only friend, we have to send a massive rescue team to bring him back. Pleaseee!

BOSS: [ THINKING… AND GETTING WORRIED ABOUT THE IMMATURITY OF RUDRA ] THAT’S ENOUGH!!!!

[ Everyone become silent, Pindrop silence. Vali immediately stands straight, his friendly demeanor gone, stepping into his role as second-in-command. ]

BOSS: RUDRA, you are a Terminal Cadet in our group. You were in the scene. Calm down, and analyze the situation. Sending a full rescue team from here and alerting everyone for one person is not how we work. Think about our resources. THINK!!

RUDRA: Yes sir!! [ THINKING HARD ]

BOSS: [ SPEAKING STERNLY ] Always think, RUDRA, no matter who is around or WHAT kind of situation you are in. The moment you STOP thinking for yourself, you will be ALL GONE.

RUDRA: Yes sir!! [ RUDRA PULLS OUT HIS OWN TERMINAL AND STARTS TRACING THE CONNECTION ] Sir… Boss, when my friend got caught, his connection died. But the thing that jammed his connection left a footprint.

Vali: Let me see that. [ VALI STEPS IN TO REVIEW THE TRACE, HIS AUTHORITY CLEAR ] You’re right. It’s a surveillance truck.

RUDRA: WHAAAAT?! Yes! A surveillance truck. It’s only 15 kilometers away… [ SHOUTING WITH EXCITEMENT ] near the margin. AHHH! I found him! All we need is one person, I will go.

Vali: The symbol is SANDCLOCK. [ COMPLETELY SHOCKED ]

BOSS: [ SMILING and REALIZING ] Understood.

Amar: [ SHOCKED ] WHAAAAT?! SANDCLOCK?! Boss, they are a completely unknown group! Never been in any book. And they have high-level tech out there?

BOSS: [ CALM ] I know, Amar. That path through the Graveyard leads to the devastated country. No one is supposed to be there. But they are. That is why I accepted your request so quickly.

Vali: [ LOOKING AT BOSS ] You… you used this entire emergency just to trap them?

BOSS: [ CALMLY SMILING ] A problem is just an opportunity wearing a mask, Vali.

Amar: [ THINKING TO HIMSELF! OHHH… He is brilliant. He used my request to make a move on the enemy board…]

BOSS: Yes. I needed to capture that new surveillance truck without making noise. We need to examine it.

RUDRA: Yes, All we need is one person, I will go.

BOSS: Wait. You aren’t going anywhere. Vali, Martin is already positioned near the enemy side. Get him on the line.

Vali: ON IT!!

Vali: Hey, MARTIN!

Martin: AHHH! WHAT’s up, Vali!! Did BOSS let you go that easy, that you called me hah!! I’m right in the middle of a surveillance sweep.

BOSS: I am LISTENING MARTIN!

Martin: WHAAAAT?! BOSSS!! I am sorry, VALI didn’t tell me this. [ MARTIN’S TONE INSTANTLY SHIFTS FROM JOKING TO STRICT MILITARY HIERARCHY ] Standing by for orders, sir!

[ RUDRA is stunned with the tone Martin has changed the moment he knew its BOSS ]

BOSS: MARTIN, you are right on the edge of enemy territory outside the Graveyard. Am I right?

Martin: YES BOSS, 100%. Running perimeter scans.

BOSS: Good! There is an unknown SANDCLOCK truck driving on the outer margin. I want you to go into their territory and take it down quietly. I need that truck… and the boy inside it… [ PAUSES FOR A SECOND ] ALIVE!!

Martin: CONSIDER IT DONE!

[ A LOUD CRASH SOUNDS ON THE MIC! BANG!! BOOM!! MARTIN IS FIGHTING FIERCELY. THEN SUDDENLY SILENCE. ]

[ Everyone in the room fell silent. For the next twenty minutes, the only sound was Vali’s fingers tapping on his screen. Rudra was biting his nails. ]

RUDRA: [ THINKING: OHHH wow… Everyone here is so cool. BOSS is right. We don’t need a big team, just the right move. ]

[ Finally, the red light on Vali’s console turned green. Vali’s phone rings. He speaks quietly, then looks to BOSS. ]

BOSS: RUDRA, your friend is fine. He will be here in few minutes. Once our examination concludes, you can bring your friend as you like.

RUDRA: AHHH! THANKS, BOSS!! I never forget this in my life time.

Amar: THANKS for accepting my request in the quick BOSS.

BOSS: Amar!! You know, I will always. You are one of us.

Vali: Not now BOSS! [ LAUGHING ] It’s a desktop Proxy NOW!! [ LAUGHING ]

BOSS: [ LAUGHING ]

Amar: UGH… BOSS!! You as well?

RUDRA: Amar, how do you know BOSS?

[ EVERYONE ARE SILENT ]

Amar: It’s a long story. And I am now a desktop guy for a reason.

RUDRA: WHAAAAT?! For a REASON? WHAT reason? Are you spying on someone?

Amar: [ HESITATED AND LOOKED AT BOSS FOR A SEC ]

BOSS: [ WITHOUT BLINKING AN EYE, LOOKING AT RUDRA WITH DEEP THINKING ]

Amar: That’s none of your concern. Your friend is here, turn around.

[ When RUDRA turn around, he saw his FRIEND walking ALIVE with some scratches, alongside MARTIN ]

Martin: Hello BOSS.

BOSS: So you of all people thinking, I am always giving Vali a work. Is that it?

Martin: Holy shit! I totally forgot about that thing I said. BOSS, HEE Sorry MY BAD.

BOSS: By the way, is everything OKAY in the MARGIN?

Martin: Graveyard MARGIN is just FINE.

[ RUDRA’S FRIEND APPROACHED BOSS ]

Friend: Thanks sir for helping.

BOSS: Say your THANKS to your friend. Its his work, you are ALIVE today.

[ RUDRA AND HIS FRIEND SEE EACH OTHER AND THEN BOSS ]

BOSS: LISTEN UP! both of you!

RUDRA and Friend: Yes Sir.

BOSS: [ SPEAKING GENTLY BUT FIRMLY ] Going to the graveyard is not child’s play. You went looking for truth, and I respect that. But this SANDCLOCK group is new. They have high-level tech. We captured their truck today because I was already watching them… but next time, you might walk into a trap we can’t save you from. Understand the board before you make a move. Be cautious.

RUDRA: Amar, I don’t know how I am going to repay this. Thanks. I’ll take my LEAVE.

Vali: Your duty is desktop guy, get going.

Amar: UGH… You still think it’s a joke? Boring..
