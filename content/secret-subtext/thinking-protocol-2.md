<i>[ 100 YEARS IN THE FUTURE... ]</i>

[ Rudra walks out of the headquarters after a long, deep security verification. He is exhausted. But deep inside, he feels a heavy wave of relief. He saved his friend, someone he thought he might never see again. Then, all of a sudden, a random voice from the crowded walking street echoes: "Think." It instantly reminds Rudra of BOSS's strict command. THINK. THINK. THINK. ]

Rudra: [ DEEP IN THOUGHT ] I can't just go home. I need answers first. Until my mind is clear, I cannot rest. 

<i>[ Rudra only has three questions burning in his mind: 
1. Who is this SANDCLOCK group with the surveillance truck? 
2. What exactly is hiding inside the Graveyard? 
And the final question... the one that might answer everything else... 
3. Who on earth is Amar now, he is complete new person for us? ]</i>

[ LATER THAT NIGHT. AFTER WORK IS DONE, AMAR IS HEADING HOME. AMAR IS A METICULOUS, HIGHLY INTELLIGENT MAN. HE ADMIRES THE BOSS'S GENIUS AND IS A LOYAL MEMBER SWORN ONLY TO THE BOSS HIMSELF. ]

Amar: [ WALKING UP TO HIS FRONT GATE, SIGHING HEAVILY ] Today was too long... UGH.

Rudra: [ STEPPING OUT FROM THE SHADOWS ] Yes sir. It is very late.

Amar: [ JUMPING BACK, COMPLETELY SHOCKED ] WHAAAAT?! RUDRA?! What on earth are you doing here?!

Rudra: I need some answers. You were the one who taught me: 'Never forget to fill the missing answers in your mind.' 

Amar: [ RUBBING HIS CHIN ] OHHH boy... Now is NOT the time, kid. Go home.

Rudra: [ STEPPING CLOSER, EYES FULL OF DETERMINATION ] I am not backing off! I saw things today that most people will never see in their lifetimes. 

Amar: AND THEREFORE?!

Rudra: THEREFORE, I want to know the full picture! Don't leave me alone in the dark!

Amar: As I said, boy, today is NOT the time!

Rudra: THEN WHEN?! 

Amar: [ SIGHS, REALIZING THE KID WON'T LEAVE ] WHAT is it that you want to know?

Rudra: How do you have such high authority in the BOSS's office? WHO ARE YOU, Amar?

Amar: [ STERN AND COLD ] I told you, I have my own reasons. And those reasons have absolutely NOTHING to do with you. 

Rudra: [ FRUSTRATED ] THEN tell me about SANDCLOCK!

Amar: They are a rogue group. They plan to take down some numbers in our territory. That is all you need to know.

Rudra: But WHY would they do that when they aren't even from this place?!

Amar: It is under investigation! I am not authorized to share ongoing operations. If your questions are over... See you next time, KID!

[ AMAR TURNS AWAY AND WALKS INSIDE. HIS MIND VOICE SAYS: "AFTER BOSS DECIDED NOT TO, I CANNOT TELL HIM ANYTHING RIGHT NOW. I AM SORRY, RUDRA." ... RUDRA STANDS ALONE IN THE DARK. HIS MIND VOICE SAYS: "SOMETHING IS WRONG HERE. AND I WILL FIND OUT WHAT IT IS." ]

<center>********* <b>NEXT MORNING</b> ********</center>

[ RUDRA COULDN'T SLEEP THE WHOLE NIGHT. NOW, IT IS TIME TO HUNT. HE GOES STRAIGHT TO NOAH'S HOUSE. NOAH IS THE EXACT SAME FRIEND WHO GOT CAUGHT IN THE SANDCLOCK TRUCK YESTERDAY. ]

Rudra: [ SENDING A TEXT MESSAGE OUTSIDE THE DOOR ] Hey Noah, how is it going buddy?

Rudra: [ WAITING FOR THE RESPONSE... TAPPING HIS FOOT IMPATIENTLY ]

Noah: [ BLINK! A TEXT COMES BACK ] Hey man! Hope you are doing okay!

Rudra: [ REPLIES INSTANTLY ] OPEN THE DOOR!! 

[ THE DOOR CLICKS OPEN. ]

Noah: Yooo! Man, you were standing here the whole time?!

Rudra: Noah!! Thank goodness you are alright! What did they do to you in the BOSS's office?!

Noah: [ SHOWING A STRAIGHT, CALM FACE ] It was just general security procedures, man. Nothing else.

Rudra: [ NARROWING HIS EYES ] Man... Are you hiding something from me?

Noah: There is nothing to hide! BOSS told me I should be completely silent about this matter.

Rudra: Yes, that's true. It's not a good idea to leak info in uncertain times like this. 

Noah: [ RELAXING HIS SHOULDERS, THINKING RUDRA UNDERSTOOD THE SERIOUSNESS ] Exactly...

Rudra: BUT... You can TELL ME! I am your best friend!

Noah: WHAAAAT?! [ WAVING HIS HANDS ] Just because you're my friend doesn't mean I will let you ruin this! 

Rudra: Yeah, right!! Come on!

Noah: [ SIGHING ] Listen, I don't know much anyway. All I saw inside that truck was a bunch of people wearing MASKS. 

Rudra: A MASK?! Huh... So they don't want to reveal themselves. BOSS might have already seen through their disguise.

[ RUDRA SUDDENLY GRABS NOAH'S SHOULDER. ]

Rudra: LET'S GO TO THE GRAVEYARD!!

Noah: [ FREEZING COMPLETELY. HIS MIND VOICE SCREAMS: "I AM GOING TO DIE THIS TIME!!" ] Listen Rudra, I understand you want to go deep into this. But I still haven't come out of the trauma from yesterday! I need some time to breathe!

Rudra: What are you talking about, Noah?! We are Terminal Cadets! If BOSS is doing his work to protect the nation, we should too!

Noah: [ MIND VOICE: "YOU GO MAN!! WHY DO I HAVE TO GO?!" ] Please Rudra, don't force me into this anymore. I'm done.

[ RUDRA IS CONFUSED. HE CONCLUDES THAT EITHER NOAH IS HIDING A BIG SECRET, OR HE IS GENUINELY TRAUMATIZED. BUT THE MYSTERY OF THE GRAVEYARD IS STILL CALLING HIM. WITH SO MANY QUESTIONS BURNING IN HIS HEAD, RUDRA GETS ANGRY. HE TAKES HIS BIKE OUTSIDE NOAH'S HOUSE AND TELLS HIMSELF: "I WILL FIND IT MYSELF!!" ]

[ RUDRA RIDES TO THE MARGIN OF THE GRAVEYARD. SURPRISINGLY, HE ISN'T ALONE. HANZO, ANOTHER CADET, WAS FORCED TO TAG ALONG. THE GRAVEYARD IS DEEPLY CALM. LITERALLY AS SILENT AS A CEMETERY. SINCE THEY ARE TERMINAL CADETS, THEY KNOW THE SECRET ROUTES TO SLIP PAST THE EDGE OF THE MARGIN WITHOUT GETTING CAUGHT. AND SO, THEY GO IN... ] 

<center>********* <b>GRAVEYARD</b> *********</center>

Rudra: They call it a GRAVEYARD for a reason, hah! Look at this place.

Hanzo: [ SHAKING IN FEAR ] NOOOO!! This is not right! We are not supposed to be here, Rudra!

Rudra: Calm down, Hanzo!! It's just us. I just want to know what is happening. 

Hanzo: It's YOU who should calm down right now, IDIOT! I told you last time, I am not willing to go in there!

Rudra: Yeah! And because you refused to come last time, Noah went with me and got caught! 

Hanzo: HEYYY! That's not my mistake! Now Noah finally understands what I tried to say. It's too dangerous here! Forget it, let's go back!

Rudra: No, Hanzo... It's just that Noah saw something. I could see it clearly on his face today.

Hanzo: WHAAAAT?! [ PANICKING MORE ] Now I am even MORE scared!! Come on, Rudra, let's go back to our patrol positions!

Rudra: It's... It's just that... when I saw Noah today, he was pale. He was terrified. And he completely refused to talk to me about anything. I can't let my friend suffer like that. I need to find the source of it all! If you really care about Noah, you will help me!

Hanzo: [ SIGHING HEAVILY. HE KNOWS HE CAN'T ABANDON THEM. ] OHHH MANNN... Looks like I have no choice here, hah! Let's do this, Rudra.

[ BOTH RUDRA AND HANZO WALK PAST THE GRAVEYARD PASSAGE. IT IS A PITCH-DARK, DUSTY PASSAGE THAT LEADS STRAIGHT TO A COUNTRY THAT WAS BURNED TO ASHES OVERNIGHT. RUDRA KNOWS EXACTLY WHICH LOCATION HE HAS TO REACH. BOTH WALK FORWARD WITH EXTREME CAUTION. ]

Rudra: How can people stand in this pitch darkness just for surveillance? What kind of grudge do they hold against us?

Hanzo: Hey man, do you know how long this will take? Because I am kind of fainting a little bit here...

Rudra: Hold your scaredy-cat mind together for a while! Just think, we are Terminal Cadets! We are strong!

Hanzo: Whatever... Did Noah really get caught in this awful place?! I feel so sorry for him...

[ BANG!!! ]
[ ALL OF A SUDDEN, A HIGH-SPEED BULLET HITS THE TREE RIGHT NEXT TO THEM! WOOD SPLINTERS FLY EVERYWHERE! ]
[ BECAUSE RUDRA AND HANZO ARE HIGHLY TRAINED CADETS, THEY INSTANTLY DIVE FOR COVER. THEY PULL OUT THEIR WEAPONS AND PREPARE FOR A FIGHT. ]

Rudra: Hanzo! Are you alright?! 

Hanzo: As of now... yes! The shooter is not trained very well, I guess!

Rudra: [ SCANNING THE DARKNESS ] Anyway... WHO IS THIS?! I don't see anyone!

Hanzo: IT'S A SNIPER!!

Rudra: You've got to be kidding me! Why would a sniper attack us in this abandoned place?! Who is this guy?!

Hanzo: It's time to HUNT, Rudra! On your west, check 2 o'clock! INCOMING!

Rudra: UNDERSTOOD!! 

[ RUDRA TAKES OUT HIS SPECIALIZED WEAPON—PINAKA. IT IS A HIGH-LEVEL, DYNAMIC GUN BUILT WITH AN ADVANCED AI SYSTEM THAT COMMUNICATES DIRECTLY WITH THE USER. ]

Rudra: PINAKA!! [ THE AI INSTANTLY ATTACHES AND BOOTS UP ] Find the target!

(WEAPON) PINAKA: [ MECHANICAL VOICE ] TARGET POSITIONING... TARGET LOCKED.

Hanzo: [ LOOKING AROUND IN HORROR ] Rudra... Look around! There are so many of them... We are surrounded!

Rudra: WHAT?! It doesn't matter! I will shoot them all!

Hanzo: That's not gonna work!! Don't be reckless! 

[ HANZO TAKES OUT HIS OWN WEAPON—BLOODHOUND. IT IS A SPECIALIZED HIGH-TECH DEVICE BUILT TO SENSE, TRACK, AND MONITOR ENEMIES PERFECTLY. ]

Hanzo: We can't just shoot blindly! Think of an alternative, CAPTAIN! I will run my scan.

Rudra: Man, this is boring... PINAKA, convert into multi-faced formation! Enemies are all around us!

(WEAPON) PINAKA: I WILL CALCULATE THE ENEMY NUMBERS WITH MY SENSORS. CAPTAIN, IT IS TRUE. WE HAVE NOWHERE TO RUN.

Rudra: I know that, you idiot! PREPARE TO SHOOT!

(WEAPON) PINAKA: ON IT. ALL WEAPONS SET.

Hanzo: We only get one clean shot at this! Do it properly, Rudra! Once they get too near, we are completely done for!

Rudra: Why don't YOU help a little, hah?! 

Hanzo: Yeah, I know, I know! Working on it! BLOODHOUND!! Analyze the perimeter with utmost accuracy!

(WEAPON) BLOODHOUND: [ BEEPING ] SCANNING IN PROGRESS...

[ SHOOT!! SHOOT!! SHOOT!! SHOTS FIRE FROM ALL SIDES! ]

Hanzo: I got the update, CAPTAIN! There are 12 enemies approaching from all directions! It's a CLOCK FORMATION! 

Rudra: That's good news! PINAKA... Fix on all 12 targets, and hold for my command!

(WEAPON) PINAKA: ALL TARGETS LOCKED. READY.

Hanzo: Wait, Rudra!! The enemies are moving faster now! We need to change the strategy! An all-out attack won't do any good!

Rudra: Which direction is the closest?! It's so dusty and windy I can't see anything!

Hanzo: On your left! 7 o'clock is the closest one!

Rudra: PINAKA! You heard the guy, DO IT!

(WEAPON) PINAKA: LOCKED TARGET. SHOOTING CONFIRMED. 
[ BOOOOOM!!! A MASSIVE BLAST ECHOES. ]

Hanzo: Everyone seems to be moving faster now... They are rushing us!

Rudra: DIRECTION?!

Hanzo: ALL AT ONCE!! 

[ RUDRA STARTS FIRING WILDLY INTO THE DEEP, WINDY DARKNESS! EVERY SINGLE DIRECTION IS EXPERTLY MANAGED BY HANZO'S TRACKING. THEY WORK TOGETHER AS A PERFECT TEAM. EVERY SHOT IS TERRIFYINGLY ACCURATE. RUDRA'S PINAKA SHOOTS THROUGH THE ENEMIES' CHEST ARMOR LIKE IT IS NOTHING! HE SHOOTS DOWN 7 OF THEM! ]
[ BUT THE REMAINING 5 ENEMIES SUDDENLY LEAP FROM THE SHADOWS, TARGETING RUDRA ALL AT ONCE! HANZO FEELS THE HEAT AND SCREAMS. ]

Hanzo: Rudra... STAY DOWN!! All 5 are jumping at once! NOOOOO!!

Rudra: I WILL MAKE THEM PAYYY!!

[ ALL OF A SUDDEN, A FLASHING SILVER BLADE SPINS THROUGH THE AIR! SLASH! SLASH! SLASH! THE BLADE CUTS CLEANLY THROUGH ALL 5 OF THE SHOOTERS IN A SINGLE, BLINDING MOTION! ]
[ RUDRA AND HANZO FREEZE, COMPLETELY SHOCKED. ]

Hanzo: [ BREATHING HEAVILY ] Yeah, right!! I should have known...

Rudra: NOAHHHHHHHH!! 

[ THE THROWING BLADE SPINS RIGHT BACK OUT OF THE DARKNESS AND LANDS PERFECTLY INTO NOAH'S HAND. IT IS HIS SIGNATURE WEAPON—WINDSHEAR. ]

Noah: [ WALKING OUT OF THE DUST, SMIRKING ] You guys are never gonna change... ARE YOU?

Rudra: HAHAHA!! Noah! You came back! I was so worried!

Noah: It's alright. After you left my house, I had a thought about what your idiotic brain would try to do. So I came straight HERE.

Hanzo: You knew the guy too well!! Are you alright from your trauma yesterday?!

Noah: I am fine, Hanzo. Nothing to worry about. How did you end up here? 

Hanzo: You already know the answer! This idiotic Rudra forced me to come!

Noah: I see... Listen, Rudra. I don't feel anything good about this place right now. The best idea is to get back to base and report this.

Rudra: NO! I want to check this out deeper! I HAVE TO KNOW, NOAH! After everything they did to us yesterday!

Noah: MAAAAN!! ... Alright! It's your call, CAPTAIN!!

Rudra: Let's do this!!

[ NOAH, RUDRA, AND HANZO MOVE DEEPER INTO THE GRAVEYARD. HANZO KEEPS UP HIS STRICT SURVEILLANCE. EVEN IN THE THICK DARKNESS, RUDRA SPOTS ANOTHER ENEMY APPROACHING. HE SHOOTS THE GUY CLEANLY IN THE SHOULDER SO THAT HE WON'T DIE. IT'S TIME TO INVESTIGATE. ]

Rudra: What IS this place?! Hey Noah, what is the reason for all this dusty, thick wind here?

Noah: It's because of the burned country on the other end of the Graveyard. The ashes from the ruins are blowing all over the margin.

Hanzo: HEY GUYSSS! SOMETHING IS OFF! My sensory Bloodhound is getting confused. There is a strange object right next to the shooter you just dropped. What is it?! 

Rudra: There is only ONE WAY to find out! [ RUDRA STARTS SPRINTING TOWARDS THE BODY ]

Hanzo: NOOO Rudra, WAIT! 

Noah: What is it, Hanzo?! 

Hanzo: It looks kind of like an old-time hourglass... I don't know what it is doing there! 

Noah: [ EYES WIDENING IN HORROR ] A Sandclock... you mean?! 

Hanzo: Kind of!! 

Noah: SHIIITTT! RUDRA, WAIIITTT!!

Hanzo: It's... It's just a clock... right?! [ NOAH SPRINTS AFTER RUDRA ] Heyyy wait for meee!

[ NOAH AND HANZO CATCH UP TO RUDRA JUST AS HE KNEELS NEAR THE FALLEN SHOOTER. THEY ALL STARE AT THE GLOWING SANDCLOCK RESTING PERFECTLY BESIDE THE ENEMY'S HAND. ]
[ SUDDENLY, THE SAND RUNS OUT. ]
[ BOOOOOOM!!!! A MASSIVE EXPLOSION OF FIRE ERUPTS FROM THE CLOCK!! ]
[ "NOOOOOOOO!!!" ]

[ BUT IN A MILLISECOND, A GLOWING BLUE SHIELD INSTANTLY EXPANDS IN FRONT OF THEM! THE SHIELD COMPLETELY BLOCKS THE BURNING FIRE AND THE SHOCKWAVE! ]
[ THE SANDCLOCKS WERE TIME BOMBERS—A NEW, ADVANCED MECHANISM RUNNING ON ACCURATE SAND, INFUSED WITH AI, UPGRADING A SIMPLE HOURGLASS INTO A MASSIVE, DEADLY TIME BOMB! ]

[ ALL THREE OF THEM ARE SAVED IN THE BLINK OF AN EYE. RUDRA, NOAH, AND HANZO ARE CAUGHT SAFELY INSIDE THE GLOWING BARRIER. THEY ARE GASPING FOR AIR. ]

Noah: Where... Where did that shield come from?!

Hanzo: [ POINTING TREMBLING FINGERS ] It came from your PINAKA!

Rudra: WHAT?! PINAKA?! What the hell is that function?!

Noah: Let's get out of here!! RIGHT NOWWW!

Rudra: Wait, let's take a different route this time to avoid more traps!

Hanzo: WHY?! FINE ANYWAYS... LET'S GO!

Noah: As you wish, CAPTAIN! JUST MOVVEEE!

[ ALL THREE OF THEM STEP OUT OF THE FADING SHIELD THAT PROTECTED THEM. MAN, THIS IS CRAZY. THE SHIELD WAS SUPER STRONG. WHO MADE IT? AND HOW DID PINAKA GET THAT KIND OF UPGRADE? ]

Rudra: PINAKA! Start finding out where that shield came from!

(WEAPON) PINAKA: YES. PROCESS INITIATED.

Rudra: Everyone, take the south side! We can have a clean route there! MOVE OUT!

Hanzo: Understood!

Noah: Gotcha!

[ THEY RUN FAST THROUGH THE DUSTY WINDS. FINALLY, THEY CROSS THE MARGIN AND STEP INTO THE SAFE PERIMETER OF THEIR PATROL AREA. THEY ALL LET OUT HUGE SIGHS OF RELIEF. ]

Hanzo: We are SAFEEE!

Rudra: No... Who upgraded my weapon? It can't just do that all on its own...

Noah: It must have been those Sandclock guys' doing... But why would the enemy protect us?!

[ SUDDENLY, A DEEP, STRICT VOICE ECHOES FROM THE SHADOWS. ]

Amar: A BRAT LIKE YOU WILL NEVER CHANGE, HUH, RUDRA?!

[ AMAR STEPS OUT OF THE DARKNESS, CROSSING HIS ARMS. ]

Rudra: Amar... What are you doing here?!

Amar: [ CLEARING HIS THROAT LOUDLY ] GUUHHH! May I ask you that question first?!

Rudra: We... We are assigned to surveillance duty today! So, we were just patrolling the MARGIN... 

Amar: ARE YOUUUU?! DO NOT LIE TO ME! I KNOW exactly where you three were...
